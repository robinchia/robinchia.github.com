    1. firefox:可直接设置

    PLAIN TEXT CODE:

    1. option.text = 'foooooooo';
    2. ie:只能设置

    PLAIN TEXT CODE:

    1. option.innerHTML = 'fooooooo';

    删除一个select的option

    1. firefox:可以

    PLAIN TEXT CODE:

    1. select.options.remove(selectedIndex);
    2. ie7:可以用

    PLAIN TEXT CODE:

    1. select.options[i] = null;
    2. ie6:需要写

    PLAIN TEXT CODE:

    1. select.options[i].outerHTML = null;
